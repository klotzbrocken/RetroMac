The .svg sources were rasterized to PNG (@1x = 21×21, @2x = 42×42) with
nearest-neighbour scaling to preserve the crisp pixel art. States:
  <name>.png (normal), <name>-hover.png, <name>-active.png (pressed)
  names: close, min (minimize), max (maximize), restore
